Our Mission

To inspire co-existence between stray animals and the people of Mumbai, by Finding loving homes for the helpless ones and creating compassion, tolerance and humanity for those on the streets. Co-exist.

Our Vision

One day every stray animal in India will be treated with love, dignity and compassion and will Find a place in our homes, hearts and lives. Co-Exist.
